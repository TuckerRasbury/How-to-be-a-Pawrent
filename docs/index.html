How This Works

What this is: A care guide for Sunny and Luna, built for anyone watching them — whether that's you at 7am or a dog sitter who just walked in the door. Everything you need is here. You don't need to install anything or know how to use an app.

Using the Today page

  1. Open the app. The What's Next block at the top tells you exactly what to do right now for each dog.
  2. Below that is the full day's schedule — both dogs, in order.
  3. Tap any schedule block to expand the full instructions for that task.
  4. Experienced caretakers: scan and skip. New caretakers: tap everything.

WFH / Office toggle

The small pill at the top of the screen says WFH or Office. Set it once in the morning based on whether the owner is home or away. It changes Luna's schedule — office days require midday coverage. When set to Office, a reminder banner appears at the top.

Reading a schedule block

Each block shows the time, which dog it's for, and the task. A NON-NEGOTIABLE badge means this task cannot be skipped or delayed — it affects Luna's training or Sunny's health directly.

Tap the block to see the full instructions.

Finding emergency info

There is a red 🚨 button in the bottom-right corner of every page. Tap it once for vet numbers, owner numbers, and what to do in specific situations. You can also reach it from the Emergency tab in the bottom navigation.

Who to call

Owner contacts are on the Emergency page. When in doubt, call. They would rather get a "just checking" text than a surprise.

A note about Luna

Luna is a young dog in active training. The protocols on her page — especially the biting protocol and crate schedule — are not suggestions. They are part of her development, and consistency across every caretaker is what makes them work. One person doing it differently sets back everyone's progress. Please follow them exactly.

Adding photos

  1. Rename your photo: sunny-1.jpg or luna-1.jpg (increment the number for each new one)
  2. Drop it into the /images folder in the GitHub repo
  3. Push to GitHub — it appears automatically in the carousel, gallery, and Today page background
